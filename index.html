Why is it better to buy in navostroki?

As a rule, housing in a completely new or under construction house will be cheaper than an apartment in a rented house (for the same area and the same square footage). It turns out that the purchase of an apartment in a new building is simply more profitable. And, of course, at the earliest stages of construction, the price is less than at the later stages. In addition, developers often attract buyers with various discounts and special conditions.New homes are usually generously equipped with CCTV cameras, alarm systems and fire safety systems. And all other systems, engineering networks, etc. will be new and in close to perfect condition — otherwise the house will not be accepted by the commission. A very important point, especially if you are not a lawyer and not a specialist in real estate transactions. It is always afraid to make a multimillion—dollar deal - you would not be left without money and without an apartment.
